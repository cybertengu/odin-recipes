PIZZA!

Description

Homemade pizza that I haven't made yet, but I want to so badly.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pizza Crust
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make the sauce. Be sure to play romantic songs in the background. The sauce needs to be loved!
  2. Make the crust. Pound the dough well. Should help your reducing stress.
  3. Put the sauce on top.
  4. Put on a lot of cheese (or little amount of cheese). Isn't it clear I love cheese?
  5. Put on pepperoni, don't be shy! I doubt I will see your pizza.
