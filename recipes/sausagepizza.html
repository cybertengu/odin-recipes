Sausages are so good and pizza, a wonderful combo!

Description

Cooking BBQ smoked sausage and put them on this pizza. My mouth is watering and I haven't eat this before yet.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pizza Crust
  • Sausage
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook BBQ smoked sausage.
  2. Make the sauce. Be sure to play romantic songs in the background. The sauce needs to be loved!
  3. Make the crust. Pound the dough well. Should help your reducing stress.
  4. Put the sauce on top.
  5. Put on cheese.
  6. Put on sausage. If you can, make a smiley face. Can't go wrong with a smile on your pizza.
