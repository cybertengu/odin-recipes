Easy-to-make Pizza

Description

Sometimes we don't got time to make the dough, so go ahead and do this approach!

Ingredients

  • Store Pizza Sauce
  • Hotdogs buns
  • Mushrooms
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Lay hotdogs flat on a pan
  2. Put the sauce on the hotdog buns.
  3. Put on cheese.
  4. Put on mushrooms.
  5. Bake them.
